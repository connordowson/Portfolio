  • Go travelling around Europe

    HUNGARIAN PARLIAMENT BUILDING, BUDAPEST

    I went Interrailing around Europe with 2 friends in the summer of 2016. We started out in Berlin where we stayed for 4 days, and visited the Reichstag, the Berlin Wall and Brandenburg Gate. We also met up with a friend who is at University there.

    After Berlin we went to Prague for 3 days which I was pretty dissapointed by, there was a massive thunderstorm that happened when we were on a pedalo which was pretty fun though.

    Next was Budapest which was amazing, we stayed in a hostel full of Australians and went on a boat party, visited the thermal baths, and did a bar crawl of the ruin bars.

    Ljubljana was next where we stayed for 3 days, the city was really nice, and we travelled an hour to Lake Bled which was amazing.

    Finally we went to Croatia, where we went to 3 different places. We stayed in Split which was a bit touristy, then took a ferry to Hvar which was really cool. Before finally heading to Rijeka, we took a trip to Krka National Park.
